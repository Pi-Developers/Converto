Converto is a thing..

Many of you - and me - always forget to change the keyboard , then after writing a 10 lines post we just be "Damn! I forgot the Alt+Shift" then you erase and write again.. We developed this app to avoid this problem, and instantly fix your mistyped text to the correct language.

Goal from app..

To overcome one of the most common problems that face arabic-english users, and they never say that "Damn!" again.

By Using converto you will experience one of the blessings of technology.. making your everyday life easier.

Functionality & Features :

Converto is :

  • Cross Platform : no matter what your PC runs, you can use Converto.

  • Multi Lingual : coded in python, java and vb.net to provide many solutions for users with one of them not installed.

  • Easy to use : no installation or anything, use out-of-box.

  • Open source : everyone can contribute to development.

Converto Can :

  • Change mistyped English characters to Arabic.

  • Change mistyped Arabic characters to English.
